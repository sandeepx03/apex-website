The science

HOW APEX
CALCULATES RECOVERY.

Every metric APEX tracks has decades of peer-reviewed research behind it. Here's exactly how we turn raw sensor data into a number you can act on.

01
Raw PPG Signal Capture
The APEX Band shines green, red, and infrared light through your skin at 25Hz. The 6 photodiodes measure how much light returns, which changes with every heartbeat as blood volume fluctuates in your vessels.
25Hz · 16-bit · 0–65535 range
02
Motion Artifact Filtering
The 3-axis accelerometer runs simultaneously, detecting wrist movement. Any PPG sample captured during motion is automatically discarded. Only clean, still-captured signals are processed.
3-axis · real-time filtering
03
Beat Detection & RR Intervals
APEX finds each heartbeat peak in the raw PPG waveform and measures the exact time between consecutive beats — called RR intervals — with millisecond precision.
Beat-to-beat · ms precision
04
RMSSD — The Gold Standard
RMSSD (Root Mean Square of Successive Differences) is calculated from the RR interval sequence. It reflects your parasympathetic nervous system — your body's ability to recover. The same method used by elite sports scientists worldwide.
RMSSD · clinically validated
